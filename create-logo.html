How to Save as Image:

  1. Take a screenshot of your preferred logo above
  2. On Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + 4 and select the logo area
  3. On Windows: Use Win + Shift + S to capture the logo
  4. Save as PNG format
  5. The file should be under 150KB (these simple designs will be ~20-30KB)

Alternative: Text-Only Version

If you prefer, you can also use just the text "TimeFlow" without any graphics, as Google accepts text logos.
